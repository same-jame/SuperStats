Privacy Policy

Super Stats stores the following player information:

  • Uber Id (long strings of numbers that identify players)
  • Display Names (and timestamps for name changes)
  • Economy, Unit and Colour data in matches
  • IP addresses are logged by nginx and periodically cleaned out. This is to prevent abuse. They are not associated with Uber Ids.
Super Stats also stores some information about custom servers:
  • A truncated SHA-512 hash of the server's IP/hostname.
